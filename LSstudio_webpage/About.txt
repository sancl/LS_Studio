Who Are We
Hello my name is Luis. What started as a simple hobby has turned into something more of a passion for photography. I have been working on growing my skills over the years. My 9-5 has always been a hurdle in being able to put more time into photography but for 2022 I made it my goal to build my portfolio even more and put in the work towards my goal!!  I have great gear that helps me be more creative and that can help me adjust to more challenging settings like low light events or even fast paced events. IM NOT JUST A TECHNICAL PHOTOGRAPHER even though it is a very important skill to have I love combining that skill with a more creative approach. I love to get creative and to find your best angles, play around with the colors to enhance the picture and make it more appealing to the eye. One thing that makes me stand out from other photographers is that I love retouching portraits to help enhance your natural beauty with different post production tools. I love videography and have done weddings, self portrait videos and a promo videos for a couple of Small Businesses!
